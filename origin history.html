About Little Flower Public School

About Little Flower Public School (Sen. Sec) is an English Medium and Co-educational institution affiliated to C.B.S.E, New Delhi, vide No: 1630348. It was founded in April 1999 during tercentenary celebration of Khalsa Panth. The school is running under the chairmanship of S. Avtar Singh Ji, the Ex-President of Shrimoni Gurudwara Prabandhak Committee. The School education assists the students in developing those qualities which prepare them to be good and valuable citizens of the society. They are educated in such innovative ways which help them to imbibe in themselves all the physical, mental, social, moral and spiritual values which prove to be a milestone for them in order to achieve their goals in life.

The school has Medical, Non-Medical, Commerce, Humanities and Vocational streams for the students of senior secondary level and they can opt for any one of these streams.The school is recognized as a well-known institution of the district where rural students are benefitted with 70% reservation in professional Colleges.
